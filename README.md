Overview:
Project Title:
Sales Analysis Dashboard – Power BI

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across different dimensions such as product, region, segment, and discount band. It helps visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) like Total Revenue, Total Cost, Total Profit, and Profit Margin % to evaluate business performance effectively.

Objectives:
Track revenue and profit trends over time.
Compare sales performance by product, segment, and discount band.
Identify high-performing segments and underperforming areas.
Provide insights for data-driven decision-making.

Key Insights:

October recorded the highest total revenue, which was significantly higher than March.
Government and Enterprise segments generated the majority of the revenue.
Among products, Paseo and Velo were top performers.
Medium and High discount bands contributed the most to total revenue.

Tools Used:
Power BI for data visualization and analysis
Excel for data preparation

Dashboard Features:
KPI cards for revenue, cost, profit, and margin
Trend line showing monthly revenue performance
Donut chart for segment-wise revenue distribution
Treemap for product-wise sales
Bar chart for discount analysis
Text box providing insights summary
